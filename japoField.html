About Japo

Japo, once a quiet rural village, has grown into a vibrant and expanding community due to the growth of Abeokuta. It was here that SOL established its first permanent structure—a foundation for missions and transformation.

Today, Japo is more than a neighborhood. It’s a beacon of hope and spiritual awakening for many through the SOL worship center.

Our Mission-Focused Church

The only SOL worship center in Abeokuta is not just a church—it's a training ground for missions-minded believers.
