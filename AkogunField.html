The Akogun Field Villages

The Akogun field comprises six unique villages:


Akogun

Onipepeye

Labeide

Ayetoro

Ogbe Inu

Ogbe Igbore

How It All Began

In 2010, when SOL first arrived in Akogun, the people were living in deep spiritual darkness and bondage. During the outreach, God miraculously healed the Baale’s favourite wife — a powerful testimony that sparked revival.

Moved by this miracle, the Baale gave land for a church, built it with his own hands, and called it “the new dimension.” He invited others to worship Jesus, despite opposition from the village. That bold step ignited the transformation of Akogun.

Transformation & Growth

Today, by the grace of God, around 85% of the people in the Akogun field are Christians. Several churches now stand across the villages — SOL was the first to arrive here with the gospel.

  • SOL has planted two strong churches in this mission field.
  • Weekly discipleship meetings are held in Ogbe, Ayetoro, and Akogun villages.
  • The atmosphere in the villages has changed spiritually, socially, and morally.

Meet the Missionaries

Wole and Esther Ajayi currently lead the Akogun mission field. Their love, leadership, and consistency continue to impact lives for Christ across the six villages.
