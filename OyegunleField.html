Japo’s sprawling suburbs

From a rural village to a thriving community—Japo's transformation amidst Abeokuta's expansion.

Japo was once a rural village, but as the city of Abeokuta expanded, it transformed into a thriving and ever-growing community. It is here that SOL established its first physical structure.

Currently, our church in Japo is the only SOL worship center in Abeokuta. Deeply mission-focused in every aspect, our services are designed to raise missions-minded believers who are equipped to discover their individual calling in God’s Great Commission. While our faith community welcomes and teaches as many as possible about the heartbeat of the Lord—missions—our primary emphasis remains on discipling young people and children. Testimonies of transformed lives abound.

The SOL team in Japo includes Pastor David and Mrs. Toyin Dania, Hassan Mohammed, and Salometu Eunice Mohammed.
