DISCOVER THE JOURNEY OF ISHOKO MISSION FIELD

A Narrative of Faith and Community

Ishoko Mission Field is born from a story that intertwines grace and divine guidance. What began six years ago as an outreach initiative in a neighboring village unexpectedly led us to Ishoko, which now stands as the heart of our mission. Here, we serve not just one community, but a network of villages, creating a tapestry of support and spiritual growth.

Our commitment transcends boundaries, as we have established vibrant churches and discipleship centers across Ishoko, Eriki-nla, Akeju, Awonke, Otunba, Oba, and Orogbo. The establishment of a free tuition and boarding school showcases our dedication to nurturing the next generation, providing them with opportunities to thrive both academically and spiritually.

EXPERIENCE THE TRANSFORMATIVE JOURNEY OF ISHOKO MISSION FIELD

A Testament of Faith and Community Impact

Six years ago, a divine intervention marked the inception of Ishoko Mission Field, a journey rooted in faith and community empowerment. Initially intended for a different village, our path led us to Ishoko, transforming it into a vibrant epicenter of spiritual growth and outreach.

Church Establishment

Through unwavering faith, we have planted thriving churches in Ishoko and surrounding villages, fostering spiritual growth and community solidarity.

Education and Empowerment

Our free tuition and boarding school offers invaluable educational opportunities, empowering the youth to reach their full potential in a supportive environment

Community Integration

We actively engage with multiple villages, building strong connections that foster a spirit of unity and shared purpose among our communities.

Support and Guidance

Our dedicated team provides ongoing support and guidance, helping individuals and families navigate their spiritual journeys with care and compassion.

Vision for the Future

Our future is bright as we aim to expand our outreach, establishing more churches and discipleship centers, and fostering deeper connections within our growing community.

Fostering Collaboration

We promote collaboration among villages, encouraging collective efforts that enhance community welfare and create lasting change.
