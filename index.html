About

Who we are

Our Vision

Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL) is committed to taking the gospel of Jesus Christ to places where the light of Godâ€™s knowledge is dim, where the gospel has not been heard, and where the power of God is not known.

Our Mission

We honour God by engaging in Spirit-empowered evangelism, planting Christ-centered churches, and establishing socially responsible ministries such as schools, hospitals, and vocational centres in different nations.

Our Global Reach

Our mission extends beyond borders, with a special focus on the 10/40 Windowâ€”a region known as The Resistant Belt. This area is home to a large population of people who have little or no access to the gospel. It covers multiple continents and includes a variety of cultures and religious backgrounds.

The 10/40 Window includes:

  • Parts of West Africa
  • All of North Africa
  • The Middle East
  • Parts of Europe
  • Most of Asia

Through evangelism, church planting, and community-focused ministries, we seek to bring transformation and raise a generation of mission-minded believers who will take the gospel to the nations.
