Transforming Hearts and Minds for Global Missions

At Springs of Life, we believe that every believer has a vital role to play in the Great Commission. Through our GO Weekends and interactive mission-focused engagements, we’re raising a new generation of bold witnesses who carry the fire of evangelism wherever they go.

  • Be Encourage – Be inspired by testimonies and stories that ignite your passion for the lost.
  • Be Equip – Gain practical tools and training to share the Gospel with confidence.
  • Be Enlisted– tep into your calling and take your place in God’s end-time mission army.

Join us and let your journey toward purpose, power, and impact begin. Together, let's ignite a passion for missions and write the next chapter of God's story through our lives.

Equipping Kingdom Leaders to Reach the Nations

A Time for Missions is our annual flagship conference designed specifically for pastors and church leaders who are passionate about the Great Commission. This powerful gathering serves as a rallying point for mission-minded leaders across nations, equipping them with vision, strategy, and spiritual fire for global impact.

  • Engage with key leaders at our annual conference aimed at empowering pastors and church leaders with essential insights on discipleship and evangelism.
  • Each year in Abeokuta, Nigeria, the conference climaxes with a memorable Graduation Ceremony for our students at the Missions Training Centre (MTC). It’s a celebration of dedication, transformation, and readiness to be sent into the harvest field.

Join us for a life-shaping encounter that renews your mission mandate and connects you to a growing network of Gospel-driven leaders

Raising a Generation of Bold Witnesses

The Young Lions Summit is an annual mentorship forum created for young men and women who have journeyed through our Missions Training Center (MTC) or participated in GO Weekends, and are now seeking continued spiritual guidance and growth. It exists as a nurturing space where passion meets purpose, and where those who take the Great Commission seriously can be continually refined, strengthened, and equipped.

This summit is more than a gathering — it’s a mentorship hub where young leaders are:

  • Poured into through sound teaching and guidance
  • Challenged to grow spiritually and step out in faith
  • Refreshed by spirit-filled sessions that deepen their walk with God

Whether you're preparing for ministry, missions, or simply desire to walk more closely with God, the Young Lions Summit offers a refreshing and empowering environment where your heart for the harvest is both celebrated and sharpened.
