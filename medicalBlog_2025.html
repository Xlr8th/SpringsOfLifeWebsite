SOL 2025 Medical Outreach

A visitation such as never before seen in these parts

Saturday, May 24, 2025

The people of Owudekudu, Itoku, Ototo, Toolu, Alako, Debonre, Nlemo, Pakala, Adee, Ayerose, Ejire experienced a mighty visitation in the form of our annual medical outreach.

This was a powerful collaboration between SOL, Path Restorers Ministries UK, and the Care Group of Rock Foundation Church, Abeokuta. The presence of God brought salvation and extensive good works (Acts 10:38).

  • 32 medical personnel on ground
  • SOL missionaries and MTC student volunteers from 3 tertiary institutions
  • Pastor Margaret Nwankama (SOL BoT Chairperson) in attendance
  • 30 new converts (mostly Muslims)
  • 475 registered beneficiaries
  • 80 glasses distributed
  • 19 dental cases attended to
  • 100+ blood sugar and malaria tests

Ailments treated: Epistaxis, diabetes, inguinoscrotal issues, pregnancy, infertility, infectious and skin diseases, respiratory infections, arthritis/myalgia, hypertension, malaria, etc.

Clothes, snacks, and drinks were distributed. Talks on general hygiene, mental health, and preventive care were also held.

“The outreach was a tremendous reminder of the impact compassion and sacrificial service can make on underserved populations.”

The people responded with warmth and great gratitude. All glory to Jesus!
