ABOUT BENIN REPUBLIC MISSION FIELD

Reaching the Unreached in Benin Republic

The Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL) is actively advancing the gospel in the Alibori Department of Benin Republic — the country’s largest and northernmost region. Alibori borders Nigeria, Niger, and Burkina Faso, and is home to a rich tapestry of ethnic groups including the Bariba (Baatonu), Dendi, Fulani, Boko, Gurma, Kyenga, and Mokole Yoruba.

With over 867,000 residents — the majority of whom live in rural areas — Alibori presents a powerful yet challenging mission field. The city of Kandi, its capital, serves as a hub for our outreach efforts. SOL missionaries are embedded within local communities, building relationships and sharing the love of Christ in practical and spiritual ways.

Our commitment

We are committed to preaching Christ, discipling believers, and equipping locals through our Missions Training Centre (MTC). We also serve through evangelism, medical care, and skill empowerment—raising missionaries who will carry the gospel forward.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see lives and communities in Alibori, Benin Republic, transformed by the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are focused on reaching unreached tribes, raising local leaders, and establishing a strong gospel presence all over the north of Benin Republic.

Our Mission in Action

Through dedicated work in town and villages like Thuy, Fafa Koffoissa, Batia, and Gando Kossikana, we are reaching people groups who have had little to no exposure to the gospel. Whether among the Fulani, Dendi, Mokole, or newly connected Gando Fulani, our mission remains clear: to bring light where darkness once prevailed, and hope where there has been none.

SOL is committed to long-term impact through discipleship, training, and cultural understanding, trusting God to transform lives, villages, and generations.

DISCOVER THE IMPACT OF SOL MISSIONS IN BENIN REPUBLIC

A Testament of Faith and Community Impact

The Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL) is actively working in multiple communities across the Kandi region of Alibori, Benin Republic. These mission fields represent a rich blend of tribal groups, each with unique cultural and spiritual needs. SOL missionaries are faithfully sharing the gospel, discipling new believers, and serving these communities in love.

Kandi

Kandi is the capital of Alibori and serves as a strategic base for SOL's outreach. Known for its cultural diversity, Kandi is home to people from nearly all the tribal groups in the region. Missionaries here engage in broad outreach, reaching both settled communities and transient groups.

Thuy

Thuy is a township predominantly inhabited by the Dendi, Mokole Yoruba peoples and the Fulani. This field presents a vibrant mix of language and tradition. The mission team is involved in Bible teaching, youth outreach, and one-on-one discipleship.

Batia

Batia is a Fulani community where SOL missionaries are building relationships with pastoral families. The Fulani people are traditionally nomadic, and ministry here focuses on consistent engagement, trust-building, and culturally sensitive gospel sharing.

Gando Kossikana

newer mission field, Gando Kossikana is home to the Gando Fulani — a lesser-reached sub-group. Early contact has opened doors for future ministry and church planting. This area represents an exciting new frontier for the gospel in Alibori.

Fafa Koffoissa

community brings together Dendi, Mokole, and Fulani tribes in a single location. Ministry here involves overcoming language and cultural barriers to foster unity through Christ. SOL missionaries run outreach programs that focus on both spiritual growth and practical needs.

In each of these locations, SOL’s heart remains the same: to make Christ known, disciple new believers, and empower communities to thrive in the light of God’s love.

Missions Training Centre Benin (MTC)

The Missions Training Centre (MTC) is a dynamic initiative of Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL), focused on equipping the clergy and the laity for cross-cultural evangelism and mission work. The center offers two main schools:

  • MTC School of Evangelism and Discipleship (MTC-SED) Equipping laity and Christian workers with the tools for impactful missions and evangelism at the basic level. As local Evangelists, Disciple makers, Oral Missionaries
  • MTC School of Leadership and Missions (MTC-SLM) Training university graduates, Pastors of Churches, Evangelists for cross-cultural minis and leadership, preparing them for roles such as: Intercultural church planters, Mission pastors, Strategy coordinators,Non-resident

Whether you’re in the marketplace, academia, or ministry, MTC prepares you to carry the gospel wherever God sends you.
