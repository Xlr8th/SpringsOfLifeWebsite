About SOL Outreaches

The Springs of Life Global Evangelistic Ministry (SOL) Outreach Division is the frontline arm of our mission to bring the love and truth of the gospel to every corner of the world. With deep compassion and a firm commitment to the Great Commission, SOL Outreach enters villages, towns, and nations—especially underserved and unreached regions—with the transforming power of God's Word.

Our work is holistic. We not only preach the gospel but also demonstrate the love of Christ through practical acts of kindness—medical care, clothing, food supplies, clean water, and empowerment programs. These activities open hearts to the message of salvation and help build trust within communities.

From gospel crusades that spark spiritual revival, to medical outreaches that restore health, and welfare missions that bring hope to the impoverished, every effort is part of our vision to see lives changed and communities transformed.

Together, we are raising an army of hope—touching hearts, saving souls, and building futures.

Share Hope Through Outreach Initiatives

Join SOL Outreaches in our commitment to sharing the love of God through hands-on support in communities worldwide. Our diverse outreach programs offer spiritual enrichment alongside vital health and welfare services, ensuring that every individual receives care that touches both body and soul.

  • Engage in transformative gospel crusades that ignite faith and inspire change.
  • Support our dedicated medical teams providing essential health services and free surgeries to those in dire need.
  • Contribute to welfare initiatives that provide vital resources like clothing and nutrition for children facing hardship.
  • Be part of sustainable projects that enhance community infrastructure, such as clean water access through boreholes and wells.

SOL 2025 Annual Medical Outreach

May 24, 2025

On Saturday 24th May, 2025, the people of Owudekudu, Itoku, Ototo, Toolu, Alako, Debonre, Nlemo, Pakala, Adee, Ayerose, Ejire and nearby villages experienced a mighty visitation in the form of our annual medical outreach, held in Owudekudu.

This effort was a collaboration between SOL, Path Restorers Ministries UK, and the Care Group of Rock Foundation Church, Abeokuta. The result was the tangible presence of God, bringing both salvation and good works (Acts 10:38).

  • 32 Medical Personnel, plus SOL Missionaries and MTC Student Volunteers
  • 30 people gave their lives to Christ (majority Muslims)
  • 475+ attendees, including unregistered villagers
  • 80 glasses distributed
  • 19 dental cases handled
  • 100+ blood/malaria tests conducted

Clothing, wares, snacks, and drinks were shared freely. Health talks on hygiene, mental health, dental and preventive care made a lasting impact.

“The outreach was a tremendous reminder of the impact compassion and sacrificial service can make on underserved populations.”
