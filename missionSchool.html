About SOL Schools

Our free tuition schools go by the name New Life Academy and in some locations, Qclass Academy. We currently operate in rural communities such as Owudekudu and Ishoko villages, where quality education is scarce but deeply needed.

These schools were founded to provide completely free, Christ-centered education to children in underserved areas. Through the commitment of missionary teachers and the support of kind-hearted partners, we are raising a new generation equipped with knowledge and values.

New Life Academy, Owudekudu

Ten years ago, classes began in open air with no buildings, shoes, uniforms, or textbooks — just the eager children of new converts. Today, the academy has grown tremendously, graduating four sets of Primary Six students and educating a current student population of 138 pupils.

This progress has been made possible through the sacrifices of faithful volunteers and missionary teachers who give their all to ensure both academic excellence and spiritual growth in every child. We are deeply grateful to everyone who has made this possible. May the Lord bless and reward your labor in Jesus' name.

New Life Academy, Ishoko

The dream for an all-boarding, tuition-free school came alive despite earlier fears of provision. Heavy rains and farming seasons made consistent attendance difficult — leading us to start the boarding house.

Today, New Life Ishoko serves 68 students and growing. We are incredibly thankful for the support of our trustees, partners, and friends who have embraced our shared vision to bring quality education to the unreached.
